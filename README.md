# WebReg2Cal

Transform your WebReg schedule into calendar events seamlessly! The WebReg2Cal Chrome extension automates the process of scheduling your university classes and exams in Google Calendar.

## Description
This repository contains the software for `WebReg2Cal`, a tool that automatically creates calendar events on your Google Calendar based on your WebReg schedule.

### Languages Used
- JavaScript (81.4%)
- CSS (13.1%)
- HTML (5.5%)

## Features
- Upload your WebReg screenshots or text for processing.
- Parse your academic schedule in seconds.
- Export your schedule effortlessly to `.ics` format for Google Calendar, Outlook, and more.

## Usage

### How to Use
1. Select the quarter you want to export.
2. Upload your schedule screenshot or paste schedule text.
3. Verify detected events and fix any OCR mismatches.
4. Export your schedule into `.ics` format.

### Example Screens
- **Select Quarter:** Choose the quarter and upload inputs.
- **Export Schedule:** Download your calendar events.
- **OCR Accuracy:** Fine-tune your schedule details.
